# LB-2026-02 — The Encryption of Operational Technology: A Protocol Landscape

A Liscere Briefing. This is a **landscape study**: it is built entirely from
published sources — peer-reviewed measurement studies, standards documents, and
industry publications — all cited in the briefing's References section.

By design it contains **no experimental evidence and no code**: there is no
`evidence/` or `code/` folder and no figures. Every load-bearing claim in the
briefing is labelled by the strength of its evidence (measured, reported,
vendor-claimed, or inferred).
